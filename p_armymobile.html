Army.mil Mobile

www.army.mil/mobile/homepage/

The Project

Army.mil's mobile support was constrained to an iOS app and an ancient WAP-friendly mobile site. A future-friendly template for using Army.mil's most important features was needed.

What I Did

I designed a new mobile homepage, article template, search page, media browser, and navigation scheme.

What I Learned

So far this is the most extensive piece of mobile work I've had the chance to take on and I relished the opportunity. Designing for touch and small-high PPI screens was a fresh challenge and I carry the experience with me, attempting to make my designs mobile friendly wherever possible.
