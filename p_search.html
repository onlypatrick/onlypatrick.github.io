Army.mil Search

The Project

Army.mil's search function is ready for a major update. We aim to improve the interface while adding functionality.

What I Did

I designed an updated search interface for Army.mil, including recommended links with the results and hooks in the right column to give a preview of results for other media types. The design hasn't yet been fully implemented but it's coming.

What I Learned

I was able to further explore form design following my work on CORE V.2. I took the opportunity to examine various search engines and take inspiration from their successes.
