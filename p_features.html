The Project

I aimed to tie together the many diverse Army.mil microsites under a single brand, with a hub page and a shared cross-site navigational bar.

What I Did

This was an idea that I had on my mind for awhile. While Army.mil has a ton of great microsites, they were like isolated islands with little to no cross-site navigation. I proposed the idea, organized the sites, and designed both the Features hub and navigational bar.

What I Learned

It's extremely rewarding to have an idea, see it through, and then watch it succeed through continual use in the years that follow.
